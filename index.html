Часто задаваемые вопросы

Ответы на самые популярные вопросы о нашем сервисе

How much does a UGC video cost? ▼
Price depends on project complexity and creator popularity. Simple video (up to 30 seconds) costs from $30, standard (1-2 minutes) - from $80, premium (up to 5 minutes) - from $150. Rush orders are charged with 50% surcharge.
How long does order completion take? ▼
Standard completion time is 3-5 business days. Rush orders available for completion in 24-48 hours with surcharge. Exact timelines are specified by each creator in their profile.
What guarantees do you provide? ▼
We guarantee money back if creator doesn't complete order on time without valid reason. We also provide up to 3 free revisions. Payment to creator happens only after you confirm work quality.
Can I use the video for advertising? ▼
Yes, after payment you receive full rights to use the video for commercial purposes. You can post on social media, website, advertising campaigns without time restrictions.
How to choose the right creator? ▼
Look at portfolio, specialization, rating and reviews. Pay attention to quality of previous work and match with your niche. Our managers can also help select the right creator.
